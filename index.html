Why dogs?

Although cats are cool, dogs are too! They are referred to as "mans best friend" for a reason.

Dogs Help You Physically

Dogs always want to go for walks. This keeps you active and in healthier condition than you'd be without all the walks.

Dogs Help You Mentally

Dogs love you always! With that love they will help you through many mental situations that can arise through out your life.
